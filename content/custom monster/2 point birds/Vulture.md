**Beaky Buzzard**

_Medium beast, chaotic neutral_

**Armor Class** 14 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points** 36 (8d6 + 8)  
**Speed** 10 ft., fly 30 ft.(he flys really slowly and only while saying the budump badump)

**STR** 14 (+2)  
**DEX** 16 (+3)  
**CON** 14 (+2)  
**INT** 6 (-2)  
**WIS** 12 (+1)  
**CHA** 16 (+3)

**Skills** Perception +3, Stealth +5  
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13  
**Languages** understands Common but cannot speak it

**Challenge** 2 (450 XP)

**TRAITS**

**Mother's Voice (Recharge 5-6).** Beaky Buzzard has a peculiar relationship with his mother. At any moment, Beaky can let out a loud, frantic yell: "MOTHER?! WHERE ARE YOU?!" This shout has a 15-foot radius and causes all creatures within range to make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be deafened until the end of their next turn. Creatures who succeed on the saving throw are not deafened but are startled, suffering disadvantage on their next attack roll or ability check.

**Keen Sight.** Beaky Buzzard has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. He can spot creatures or objects up to 1 mile away in clear conditions, especially if theyâ€™re making any movement.

**Flighty.** Beaky Buzzard can Dash, Disengage, or Hide as a bonus action. He has advantage on Dexterity saving throws made to avoid falling.

**Disruptive Yell.** As a bonus action, Beaky can screech in a way that rattles creatures nearby. Each creature within 10 feet must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or suffer disadvantage on their next attack roll.

---

**ACTIONS**

**Beak Peck.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 7 (1d6 + 4) piercing damage.

**Feather Storm (Recharge 6).** Beaky Buzzard can release a flurry of his own feathers, making a chaotic mess in the air. Each creature within a 15-foot cone must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the creature takes 14 (4d6) slashing damage and is knocked prone. On a successful save, they take half damage and are not knocked prone.

**A Flurry of Wings (Recharge 5-6).** Beaky Buzzard flaps his wings so violently that they create a gust of wind. Each creature within 20 feet must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be pushed 15 feet away from Beaky. Any creature that is pushed into an obstacle takes 7 (2d6) bludgeoning damage.
